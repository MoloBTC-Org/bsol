# History of the Bitcoin Sovereign Open License (BSOL)

## Origination of v1.0

Version 1.0 of the Bitcoin Sovereign Open License (BSOL) and its companion Sovereign Bitcoin Knowledge Declaration were developed through structured collaboration inside this Grok (xAI) workspace.

The work was initiated and stewarded by Jacques Strydom, PMP (Jabulani Jakes) under MoloBTC-Org. All documents, licensing logic, philosophical framing, and publication structure were produced through iterative computational collaboration with Grok by xAI. This approach was chosen deliberately over standalone document production in order to achieve higher structural rigor, version control discipline, and long-term maintainability.

The collaboration enabled the project to move from conceptual sovereign licensing principles to a complete, self-consistent v1.0 release ready for community propagation and hard-copy distribution.

Subsequent updates will be recorded here as the project evolves through the same open processes.
