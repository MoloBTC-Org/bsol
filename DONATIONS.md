# Donations

Thank you for considering support for the stewardship of the Bitcoin Sovereign Open License (BSOL) v1.0 and the MoloBTC project.  
All donations are voluntary and represent direct value exchange for the effort in maintaining and propagating sovereign Bitcoin knowledge and tools. No obligations are created.

## Direct Donation Methods Available

- **Bitcoin (BTC) on-chain**: Send to the current stewardship Bitcoin address. The address is maintained in [.github/FUNDING.yml](.github/FUNDING.yml) and updated on the official page.
- **GitHub Sponsors**: Recurring or one-time sponsorships through the GitHub Sponsors platform for this repository (convenient recurring support option).

## Official Stewardship Page for Current Details

For the most up-to-date addresses, any Lightning options (if enabled), additional methods, and how support is used, please visit the official MoloBTC stewardship page:  
https://www.MoloBTC.africa

## For Projects Using This License (Pass-Through)

If you are publishing your own work under BSOL v1.0, you are encouraged to include your own donation information in your project’s documentation or README. This supports your specific efforts while the core license remains unchanged.

All contributions help sustain the work of ordinary hands in service of ungovernable openness and sovereign Bitcoin tools.
